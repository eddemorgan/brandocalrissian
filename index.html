Producer. Composer. Student of the craft.

Brando Calrissian is a producer and composer based in London. He grew up swallowing every genre he could find, metal to jazz to country to classical to hip hop, and never picked a lane. He specializes in lofi hip hop but makes electronic, ambient, synthwave, orchestral, and vaporwave whenever the mood pulls him there. He started performing in middle school, started composing his senior year, and has been releasing music relentlessly ever since. He's now studying Music & Sound Production at Ravensbourne University London, sharpening the craft while building a catalog that already runs deep.
